Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a coolroom trailer?

To rent a coolroom trailer, simply fill out the form on the "Browse Trailers" tab and submit your request. We will review your request and confirm the availability and price of your rental.

What is the cancellation policy?

You may cancel your rental up to 24 hours before the start of your rental period. Cancellations within 24 hours of the start of your rental period will not be refunded.

Do you offer delivery and pickup?

Yes, we offer delivery and pickup services for an additional fee. Please contact us for more information.
